Our story

Zenebona is about the magic that music brings into our life. This is an underground electronic music label, community that focuses on what truly matters: delivering the finest organic & deep house tracks to our audience. We believe that our music will find its way to some beautiful souls to connect on a deeper level through sounds. In the center of the Label's mission is to support young and talented electronic musicians from all around the World.
